The Horus

"The Horus" is a further light aircraft we designed in UoB integrated design course. This course aims at encouraging people from different disciplinary to work together. So we have 4 people in Mechanical engineering, 1 in civil engineering, and 2 in electrical engineering.

"The Horus" 是我们在伯明翰综合设计课程中设计一款未来轻型飞机。这门课鼓励不同学科的人一起工作。因此我们组有4位机械工程的同学，1位土木工程的同学，和2位电子工程的同学。



Selfie was shotted in the design project showcase ceremony.

图片摄于项目展示仪式。


Electricals are responsible for the power design and simulation using Matlab Simulink : generator, fuel cell power supply, landing gear drive, emergency power, and solar energy. Each module consist the design of control, power generation, converter and load. The finial design shown below.

电器工程的指责在于使用 Matlab Simulink 完成飞行器的动力设计和仿真。主要包括：发电机，燃料电池电源，起落架驱动，应急电源和太阳能。每一个模组包含相应的控制，供能，转换器和负载设计。下图展示了最终的设计。



It's a well organized and dynamic communication group. All the subsection were closely connected. The design and implement schedule followed the Gantt chart. It's definitely a satisfying experience in engineering.

整体而言，我们是一个组织严密并且充满活力的小组。设计中的所有环节都紧密相连。设计和实施都按照甘特图的预期进度进行。这绝对是一次令人满意的工程设计经历。

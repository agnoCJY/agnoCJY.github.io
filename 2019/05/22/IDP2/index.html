The Horus

"The Horus" is a further light aircraft we designed in UoB integrated design course. We have 4 people in Mechanical design, 1 in civil design, and 2 in electrical design.



Selfie was shotted in the design project showcase ceremony.


Electricals are responsible for the power design and simulation using Matlab Simulink : generator, fuel cell power supply, landing gear drive, emergency power, and solar energy.



It's a well organized and dynamic communication group. All the subsection were closely connected. The design and implement schedule followed the Gantt chart. It's definitely a satisfying experience in engineering.
